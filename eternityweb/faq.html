Why the name Eternity In A Moment?

Eternity In A Moment is a name that was inspired by a vision God had shown our founder, Ms. Wawa (aka Eunice Wachera), back in 2020, and it is backed up by the scripture of Ephesians 3:11, that says "Yet God has made everything beautiful for its own time. He has planted eternity in the human heart, but even so, people cannot see the whole scope of God’s work from beginning to end."" The name Eternity In A Moment, is inspired from all this, and communicates the reality and heart f God for his people to experience eternity in him, and not just as a time period, but as a reality outside time, a place where the life of God is expressed through us. Eternity lies in Jesus, so to experience it, you need to know him.

What do you do?

We are a platform that is set to help bring up a generation of people who need to be pointed to light to be able to express quality, lasting and creative susbstance - within the creativity space & beyond -, in various facets of life. We have a strong conviction within us to help people see the possibilities that lie in God, and build up a certain 'breed' of people who know God and excell in their various spheres of influence, courtesy of the character they have built from the mentorship, discipleship & leadership we offer.
We are currently plugged into podcasting, and are slowly expanding into the other areas of influence that we are entrusted to over time. Stay tuned for more.
